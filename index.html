Today,

I want to share with you my favourite hobby and teach you how to plant a flower bulb or a plant cutting.

It could seem quite scary at first, but I promise you, after a couple plant babies, you'll be one heck of a pro!

Bulb

  1. Put a bulb into a pot and cover it with soil.
  2. Water the pot until water drains out of the holes from the bottom.
  3. Keep the pot in sunlight and re-water the pot when all the soil dries up.
  4. Plant will grow a stem and enjoy your new baby!

Plant Cutting

  1. Find any plant that you like and make a cutting in between the plant's node, let that node sit in water to grow some roots.
  2. After cutting has grown enough roots, it is time to put the plant in soil.
  3. Water the plant and make sure to keep the soil nice and saturated for a couple days as the plant is aclimated to moisture surrounding it.
  4. Little by little, the plant will get used to dryness, where you will then follow the same steps as the bulb
  5. Plant will start to grow leaves and enjoy your new cutting!
